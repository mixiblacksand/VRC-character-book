Mio3 UV is a free extension you can get from the Preferences Extensions menu.

While it has a lot of features common in UV toolkits, two useful ones are Orient World and Auto Body Parts:

![](Pasted%20image%2020260403184244.png)

Orient World will orient the UV islands based on their axis in world space. It will quickly straighten out your islands in a way should be intuitive to someone viewing them.

Auto Body Parts will attempt to align islands from symmetrical body parts. While it's not perfect, it should get your arms and leg bits roughly aligned to ease sorting.

You'll still have to do manual placement, of course, but I find these buttons make the initial sorting much faster.
