## Quick Favorites
```
RMB on UI elements : Add to Quick Favorites
Q : Open Quick Favorites Menu
```
If we right click on most buttons or checkboxes in Blender, we are given the option 'Add to Quick Favorites.'
![[Pasted image 20260331195703.png]]

Add AutoMirror to your favorites, then in the 3D viewport press `Q`. A small menu will appear under the cursor with the AutoMirror option. You can do this with most things you find you use a lot. I'll click on the Add Modifier button, and from that menu add regular Mirror.

Then I'll go into Edit mode and add Symmetrize. If you look at the quick menu in object and edit mode, you'll notice that it is contextual to the mode you are in.
