Two more ways we can make more geometry are by selecting faces and either inserting them , adding loops while leaving them flat, or extruding them, adding loops that extend outward.
## Inset
```
Inset : I
```
Select one or more faces and press I. Drag the mouse to control how far the faces are inset.
![[Pasted image 20260331205420.png]]

If you have multiple faces selected, you can press I a second time to inset them individually.

![[Pasted image 20260331205506.png]]

## Extrude
```
Extrude : E
```

Select one or more faces and press E. Drag the mouse to pull them outward or push them inward.
