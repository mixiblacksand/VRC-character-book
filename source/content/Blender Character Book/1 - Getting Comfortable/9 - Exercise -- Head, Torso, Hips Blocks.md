
> [!Objective] Goal
> Make three blocks that roughly represent the head, torso, and hips proportion of your character.

Let's get some practice with one of the fundamentals of character art - body proportion. Exactly _what_ those proportions should be is a matter of taste. You might wanna make a nine head high superhero or a three head high chibi thing, but either way getting those three blocks dialed in is a staple of character design. 
		
Use the controls you've learned (or discovered!) so far to make three cubes and roughly move, rotate, and scale them into the proportions you might want.
